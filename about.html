About us

Our Mission

Our Vision

Our Story

2011
Established in West Africa

Launched operations in Benin & Togo, focusing on the sourcing and export of high-quality Cashew nuts. We began building relationships with local farmers, establishing a foundation for our commitment to ethical and sustainable sourcing practices. Initial efforts concentrated on developing efficient supply chains and ensuring consistent product quality.

2017
Expansion to Senegal & Tanzania

Expanded our product portfolio to include Soya beans and Sesame seeds, broadening our reach and diversifying our offerings. This expansion into Senegal and Tanzania allowed us to tap into new agricultural regions and further solidify our presence in West and East Africa. We also began to explore new markets for these products.

2018
Entered Vietnam Market

Successfully penetrated the Vietnamese markets, significantly strengthening our global trade connections and expanding our distribution network. This strategic move opened up new avenues for growth and allowed us to reach a wider customer base. We adapted our logistics and marketing strategies to cater to the specific needs of these new markets.

2023
Sustainable Sourcing Initiatives

Demonstrated our commitment to environmental responsibility by investing in eco-friendly logistics solutions and launching farmer empowerment programs. These initiatives focus on promoting sustainable farming practices, providing farmers with access to training and resources, and minimizing our environmental footprint throughout the supply chain. We believe in creating shared value and contributing to the long-term sustainability of the agricultural sector.
