Our Business spread

Worldwide Supply Chain

Our operations span across key regions, ensuring a seamless and efficient supply chain from farm gate to market. we started our business with set-ups in west Africa in 2011. Since then, we have established supply chain network in Benin, Togo, Senegal, Ivory Coast, Tanzania, India and Hong Kong.

The sourcing markets are mostly in Africa and destination markets are Europe, Middle East and Asia.

we've strategically positioned our facilities in key agricultural hubs and logistical centers around the world. This global network allows us to source the finest quality agricultural products, maintain stringent quality control, and deliver them to business worldwide with speed and reliability. Our integrated approach optimizes every step of the process, minimizing delays.
