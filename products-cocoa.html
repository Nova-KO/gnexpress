Jute bag

Jute is a crop which is sown every year. It is mainly grown in India and Bangladesh which together contribute to 90% of the world production of jute. Jute is natural fiber and is woven to make Jute Bags which is used as packing material for agricultural commodities.

Our jute bags business is focused on niche sourcing and marketing. Jute bags shares product adjacencies with our food commodity sourcing of cocoa, cashew and coffee. We source food grade jute bags from India and supply it to the West African countries and Vietnam. We also cater to the jute bags demand of the commodity industry.

Customers want the right quality jute bags with assurance of food grade quality with in time delivery. We cater to these needs by providing and ensuring FOOD GRADE quality. We also provide our customer service in terms of printing the brand names on bags, supplying different specifications as per requirement, providing timely supply by storing the bags for the clients close to delivery point.
